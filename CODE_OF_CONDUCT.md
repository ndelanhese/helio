# Code of Conduct

## Our commitment

We pledge to make participation in Helio welcoming and harassment-free for everyone, regardless of age, body size, disability, ethnicity, sex characteristics, gender identity and expression, experience level, education, socioeconomic status, nationality, personal appearance, race, caste, color, religion, or sexual identity and orientation.

## Expected behavior

- Be respectful, patient, and constructive.
- Assume good intent while addressing harmful impact.
- Give actionable technical feedback.
- Respect privacy, especially device identifiers and energy data.
- Accept responsibility, apologize, and learn from mistakes.
- Keep safety discussions factual and conservative.

## Unacceptable behavior

- Harassment, threats, discrimination, or sexualized attention
- Trolling, insults, personal attacks, or sustained disruption
- Publishing private information without explicit permission
- Encouraging unsafe inverter changes or bypassing electrical protections
- Abuse of project channels for spam or unrelated promotion

## Enforcement

Report conduct concerns privately to project maintainers through GitHub. Maintainers will review reports promptly, protect reporter privacy where possible, and apply proportionate actions ranging from a private correction to permanent exclusion.

Project maintainers may remove, edit, or reject contributions and interactions that violate this code. Enforcement decisions should explain behavior, impact, and required correction without publicly exposing sensitive report details.

This policy is adapted from Contributor Covenant 2.1: https://www.contributor-covenant.org/version/2/1/code_of_conduct.html
